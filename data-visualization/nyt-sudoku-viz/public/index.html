Let's start with my strategy

When I first see a sudoku grid, my instinct is to look for naked singles. Naked singles are cells where there can only be one possible value. (They‚Äôre also called sole candidates, but why would you call them that when you could say naked singles? I digress.)

Using the process of elimination, I identify naked singles and solve those cells. I only ever fill in a value when it‚Äôs a naked single, i.e., I never guess.
